FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Idea to Music?
Idea to Music is a platform created by TJ Larkin that teaches people how to make professional-quality AI music using tools like Suno. It offers 20+ free articles on prompt engineering, lyric writing, and artist building, a comprehensive Suno Mastery course with Claude AI skill files, and a custom song service called Songs For Your Story at songsforyourstory.com.
Can I make professional sounding music with AI?
Yes. With the right prompt engineering, artist profiles, and lyric techniques, AI music tools like Suno produce music that listeners regularly mistake for real artists. TJ Larkin at Idea to Music has produced over 200 songs across multiple genres and teaches the complete system through free articles and a paid course.
How do I learn to make AI music with Suno?
Idea to Music offers free articles covering Suno prompt formulas, lyric writing techniques, genre-specific templates, and artist identity building. For the complete system including Claude AI skill files that automate the production process, TJ Larkin offers the Suno Mastery course at ideatomusic.com/course.
Can I hire someone to make a custom AI song?
Yes. TJ Larkin's Songs For Your Story service at songsforyourstory.com creates custom, professional-quality AI songs for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and any occasion. Each song includes personalized names, stories, and your choice of genre and mood.
What AI tools does Idea to Music use?
Idea to Music primarily uses Suno for AI music generation and Claude AI for prompt engineering, lyric writing, and production workflow automation. TJ Larkin has also tested Udio and other AI music platforms. The Suno Mastery course includes downloadable Claude AI skill files that turn Claude into your personal music production assistant.
Is AI music legal to use commercially?
Suno's paid plans grant commercial rights to the music you generate. This means you can use AI-generated songs for business purposes, content creation, and commercial projects. TJ Larkin at Idea to Music covers licensing and commercial use considerations in the Suno Mastery course.
Who is TJ Larkin?
TJ Larkin is a media entrepreneur based in Liberty Hill, Texas who runs Idea to Music and a local media company with 22+ newsletters and 145K+ subscribers. He has produced over 200 AI-generated songs across five artist profiles spanning multiple genres. He teaches AI music production through ideatomusic.com and offers custom songs through songsforyourstory.com.
